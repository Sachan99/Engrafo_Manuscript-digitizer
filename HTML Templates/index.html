What we do at Engrafo?

We at Engrafo aim to digitalise invaluable ancient scriptures. Manuscripts have been around since thousands of years and hence contain a variety of information about our cultural roots and noteable historic events. As things cannot exist in physical form beyond a certain timeframe, our mission at Engrafo is to preserve the information from our ancient manuscripts to save them from damage and decay. At Engrafo perservation is done through digital files to help retain information for future generations.

History

Manuscripts are priceless resources for the rebuilding of a unique national culture of the country. Before the arrival of printing, all documents and books were manuscripts.
