How to use digitizer?

Our digitizer is very user-friendly. Follow the steps given below to digitize your scanned manuscripts.

1

Upload

Upload the scanned image files of jpeg format using the choose files button.

2

Select Language

Select the language of the scanned manuscript from the drop-down list.

3

Digitize

Click the digitize button and wait for sometime. The digitizing might take a few seconds so sit back and relax.

4

Download

After digitising a download option appears.Click on download to download your editable digital file.
